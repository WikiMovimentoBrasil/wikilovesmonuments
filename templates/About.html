Are you ready to participate in the biggest photo contest in the world?! The Wiki Loves Brasil application offers you several ways to participate: Sending your photographs of Brazilian monuments in a simplified way, geolocating monuments without registered coordinates and suggesting monuments that happen not to be on the lists.

Wiki Loves Monuments is the biggest photo contest in the world! The national stage takes place between September 1st and 30th, 2022.

Participate and compete for prizes of up to R$2500. More details on the contest page on Wikipedia.

Wiki Loves Brasil is an application developed by Wiki Movimento Brasil user group.

Doubts, complaints, suggestions or compliments: Send a message to eder.porto@wmnobrasil.org .
